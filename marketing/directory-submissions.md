**Long Description:**
Pitchable generates professional pitch decks from a brief, an uploaded document, or a topic. You configure the pitch lens — audience, goal, tone, and framework — then the AI structures your content and renders it into slides using Marp CLI. Edit any slide via natural language chat. Export to PPTX or PDF. Images generated via Nano Banana 2 (Replicate). Free tier includes 15 credits (2–3 full decks). Starter plan $29/mo, Pro $79/mo, Enterprise custom pricing.

## Submission Tips

- **Product Hunt**: Submit the night before (11pm PT) to maximize upvote window. Ask 10–15 supporters to upvote on launch day, not before. Never mass-message — PH penalizes coordinated upvote patterns.
- **HN**: Post Tuesday or Wednesday, 9–11am ET. Do not ask for upvotes. The Show HN community responds to technical honesty, not marketing language.
- **AI directories**: Submit to There's An AI For That and Futurepedia first — they drive the most referral traffic in the AI tool category.
- **BetaList**: The 2–4 week queue is real. Submit 3 weeks before your planned launch date.
- **Capterra/G2**: These drive review-based SEO. Add the listing early; reviews come over time.
