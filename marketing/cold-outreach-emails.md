## Notes on Usage

**Personalization tokens:**
- `[FIRST_NAME]` — required for all sequences
- `[COMPANY]` / `[FUND]` — required; use LinkedIn or Crunchbase to verify
- `[ROUND_SIZE]` — Sequence A only; use from public press/LinkedIn if available
- `[TRIGGER]` — Sequence A only; recent fundraising news, product launch, or hiring signal

**Sending guidelines:**
- Use a plain-text email client or tool (HEY, Gmail, Superhuman). No HTML templates.
- Send from a custom domain address (e.g., anthony@pitch-able.ai), not a personal Gmail.
- Space sequences at minimum 4–5 days apart. Never more than 3 emails per prospect.
- If a prospect replies "not interested," remove immediately. No fourth email.
- Track open/click rates; if sequence A cold reply rate is below 3% after 50 sends, rewrite A-1 subject line.

**Recommended tools:** Instantly, Lemlist, or Apollo for sequencing. Manual send for warm intros and VCs.
